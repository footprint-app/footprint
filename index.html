TPRINT

A mobile application that is dedicated towards the idea of sharing unique locations with one another and leaving your mark on this earth. The world can be a dangerous place, but that should not deter you from leaving your mark.

OUR STORY

Discovery is a word that follows us. It is a word that makes us unique and wanting to know more. Our inspiration arose from the desire to discover a world around us using this developing digital playground. There are always huge dangers in seeking out the undiscovered, but it is important to always be safe and aware of what lies ahead.

MAKE SOME FOOTPRINTS

Make an impact on the world - name unknown constellations or name your own destinations. You will be able to share the "hidden gems" of the world around you for others to see. Rate, review, record your stats, make new friends, explore, and possibly learn something new.

With high rewards, there is always risk. Most of these undiscovered locations or hidden locations are off trail. They require you to tread lands which may have been untouched by many people. We encourage you to discover, but we highly encourage you to be prepared. Our application focuses on safety and we will always make sure you are prepared for adventures to come.
